Privacy Policy

This application does not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

The app only accesses images selected by the user in order to perform image compression or resizing features.

Storage Permission

The application may request storage access in order to:

  • Select images from your device

All image processing happens locally on your device.

This information is used to provide relevant ads and improve ad performance.

We do not collect or store any personal data directly. However, third-party services like Google AdMob may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting their device settings.

For more details, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

We value your trust in using our application. Images processed by the app remain on your device and are not uploaded to external servers.
