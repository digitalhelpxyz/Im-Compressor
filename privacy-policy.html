Privacy Policy

Im Image Resize & Compress

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Im Image Resize & Compress collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. Our app provides image compression, image resizing, background removal, passport photo creation, image format conversion, and optional Premium PRO features.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address.

The application only accesses photos that you explicitly select for editing.

How We Use Your Images

Images selected by you are used only for the requested features, including:

  • Compress Image Size
  • Resize Images
  • Background Removal
  • Passport Photo Maker
  • Image Format Conversion
  • Save processed images to your device

Your images are never sold or shared with third parties.

Image Processing

Most image processing is performed locally on your device. We do not upload your personal photos to our servers for storage or marketing purposes.

Background Remover

The Background Remover feature allows users to erase or replace image backgrounds.

  • Only user-selected photos are processed.
  • Images remain under your control.
  • Processed images are saved only when you choose to save them.
  • Transparent PNG images can be exported.

Passport Photo Maker

Passport Photo Maker allows users to crop and generate passport-size photos using their own images.

  • Photos are processed only for passport photo generation.
  • No passport photos are uploaded or stored by us.
  • Photos remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

Storage & Media Permissions

The application may request permission to access your photos or storage in order to:

  • Select images
  • Compress or resize images
  • Remove backgrounds
  • Create passport photos
  • Save processed images

These permissions are used only for providing app functionality.
